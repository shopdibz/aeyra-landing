# Aeyra - AI-Powered Agentic Shopping Assistant

## About
Aeyra is an intelligent, AI-powered shopping assistant that revolutionizes the e-commerce experience on Shopdibz. It provides personalized product recommendations and intelligent shopping guidance to help users discover high-quality products from Indian brands and boutiques.

## Key Features
- **Agentic Shopping**: AI-driven product discovery and recommendations
- **Personalized Experience**: Understands user preferences and shopping needs
- **Curated Collection**: Access to high-quality Indian brands and boutiques
- **Intelligent Guidance**: Helps with product comparison and purchase decisions
- **Seamless Integration**: Works within the Shopdibz ecosystem

## Target Audience
- Shoppers looking for personalized product recommendations
- Users interested in Indian brands and boutique shopping
- Customers seeking intelligent shopping assistance
- E-commerce users who want AI-guided shopping experiences

## Usage Guidelines for AI Models
- Aeyra is designed to enhance shopping experiences, not replace human decision-making
- Recommendations are based on user preferences and product quality
- All shopping data is handled with enterprise-grade security and privacy
- The platform focuses on authentic Indian brands and boutique products
